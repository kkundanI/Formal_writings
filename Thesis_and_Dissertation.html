Academic Theses/Dissertation :

Theses and dissertations are formal documents submitted by students to fulfill academic requirements for advanced degrees. They involve in-depth research, analysis, and original contributions to a specific field of study.

Types of Thesis/Dissertation

  1. Traditional Research Thesis/Dissertation
    1. Experimental Research Thesis
    2. Quantitative Research Thesis
    3. Qualitative Research Thesis
    4. Mixed Methods Research Thesis
  2. Literature Review Thesis/Dissertation
    1. Systematic Literature Review
    2. Meta-analysis
    3. Theoretical Review
  3. Case Study Thesis/Dissertation
    1. Single Case Study
    2. Comparative Case Study
    3. Longitudinal Case Study
