Reports :

Formal reports present detailed information, analysis, and findings on a specific topic or issue. They often follow a structured format and may include an executive summary, introduction, methodology, results, conclusions, and recommendations.

Types of Reports

  1. Financial Reports
    1. Balance Sheet
    2. Income Statement
    3. Cash Flow Statement
    4. Financial Ratios
  2. Sales Reports
    1. Sales Performance Report
    2. Sales Forecasting Report
    3. Sales Conversion Report
    4. Customer Acquisition Report
  3. Marketing Reports
    1. Market Research Report
    2. Advertising Campaign Report
    3. Social Media Analytics Report
    4. Website Traffic Report
  4. Other Reports
    1. Research Report
    2. Progress Report
    3. Audit Report
    4. Feasibility Report
