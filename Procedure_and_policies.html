Policies And Procedures:

Formal policies and procedures are written documents that establish guidelines, rules, and protocols within an organization. They outline expectations, processes, and responsibilities for employees or members to follow.

Types of Policies

  1. General Policies
    1. Code of Conduct Policy
    2. Anti-Harassment Policy
    3. Attendance and Punctuality Policy
    4. Dress Code Policy
    5. Social Media Policy
  2. Administrative Policies
    1. Document Management Policy
    2. Travel and Expense Reimbursement Policy
    3. Procurement Policy
    4. Record Keeping Policy
    5. Meeting Management Policy
  3. Human Resources Policies
    1. Recruitment and Selection Policy
    2. Performance Management Policy
    3. Leave and Time Off Policy
    4. Employee Benefits Policy
    5. Grievance and Complaint Policy
  4. IT and Security Policies
    1. Information Security Policy
    2. Acceptable Use Policy
    3. Data Backup and Recovery Policy
    4. Software and Hardware Procurement Policy
    5. Incident Response Policy
